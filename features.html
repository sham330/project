What is TRKDispatch Software and What Will it Do?

TRKDispatch offers web-based, cost-effective, and user-friendly truck dispatch software that scales with your business. Whether you're managing one truck or a fleet of fifty or more, our solution provides enterprise-level functionality at an affordable price point. Access everything through your web browser with no installation required.

Web-Based Platform

Completely web-based system accessible from your office, on the road, or anywhere else. Use all features directly from our website with nothing to install.

Intuitive Design

Simple, user-friendly design allows you to start using the program immediately, with free training and support available when needed.

Load Management

Assign, change, or reassign trucks to loads with ease. Create detailed load sheets for your drivers and manage active loads efficiently.

Fleet Management

Maintain detailed information about carriers, drivers, and multiple trucks. Track statistics and manage your entire fleet from one dashboard.

Advanced Reporting

Generate detailed reports by driver or customer, view carrier statistics including volume and revenue, and track load and expense statistics.

Payroll & Invoicing

Easy payroll functions with pay by miles or percentage, detailed driver pay stubs, and customer invoices that can be printed with a single click.

Paperless Operations

Save all paperwork digitally and access your documents anywhere with a click. Go completely paperless with secure cloud storage.

Unlimited Users

Affordable pricing plans with access granted to unlimited users. Scale your team without worrying about additional user costs.
