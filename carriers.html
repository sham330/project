Truck Dispatch Software Features for Carriers

TECK USA Inc. has designed comprehensive truck dispatch software that is completely web-based and accessible from anywhere - whether at home, in your office, at truck stops, or over the road. Our solution features a simple interface design that allows you to quickly understand and use the program with minimal learning curve.

Web-Based Access

Completely web-based platform accessible from anywhere - your home, office, truck stops, or on the road. All features are available directly from our website with nothing to install.

Easy Dispatch Management

Our intuitive dispatch board allows you to view and manage all active loads, whether your own or those posted throughout your company. Easy preview function for load details anytime, anywhere.

Quick Load Duplication

Quickly duplicate loads without wasting time re-doing data entry. Create loads and have them prepared for when your drivers arrive, streamlining your operations.

Driver Portal Access

Drivers can view their loads and check their payment status or history directly from the carrier portal, reducing administrative burden and improving communication.

Integrated Payroll System

Easy, fully-integrated driver payroll calculated by percentage. Create year-end history for any driver quickly in just minutes, simplifying your accounting processes.

Professional Invoicing

Create detailed invoices to brokers and print them with a single click. Professional formatting and comprehensive details ensure faster payment processing.

Key Benefits for Carriers

This is only a partial list of what TECK USA Inc. truck dispatch software can do to help you quickly and easily manage your business. More features are being added regularly based on user feedback.

Free Training & Support: We provide comprehensive free training and ongoing support to ensure you get the most out of your dispatch software.

No Installation Required: Everything runs directly from your web browser - no software to install, update, or maintain on your computers.

Simple Interface Design: Intuitive design means you can start using the program immediately without extensive training or learning curves.

Real-Time Load Management: View and manage all active loads in real-time, whether they're your own or posted throughout your company network.

Efficient Data Entry: Quick load duplication features eliminate repetitive data entry, saving valuable time in your daily operations.

Driver Self-Service: Drivers can independently check load assignments and payment status, reducing administrative calls and improving efficiency.
